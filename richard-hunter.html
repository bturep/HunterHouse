Hunter

Richard Morrow Hunter — architect, trained at the University of Colorado and the University of Oklahoma. Practice in California and Alaska before relocating to Victoria, British Columbia, in 1968. His residence at 203 Goward Road occupied him from 1970 until 2020 — five decades of continuous design on a single site. b. Phoenix AZ · d. 14 January 2023.

Portrait

The Hunter Residence is the most extensively documented project in his archive. Across its fifty years, Hunter remained its sole architect, sole client, and sole resident. The working file he kept of its drawings is the principal subject of this Foundation.
