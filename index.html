The Hunter Residence and its archive.

The Hunter House Foundation stewards the residence Richard Hunter designed for his family at 203 Goward Road, Prospect Lake, and revised continuously across more than five decades. The archive catalogues the drawings, photographs, and related materials that document this fifty-year working record.

The Foundation

The Hunter House Foundation stewards the residence and the archival legacy of its architect. Its overarching aim is to realize the speculative futures embedded in Hunter's drawings — among them a cantilevered Skywalk and the unfinished East Wing developments — and to open the archive to scholars, students, and the public.

Source & data

The archive is maintained as a structured database — every item recorded with a permanent identifier, a controlled vocabulary, and links to related works, places, and people. The data is open and queryable.
