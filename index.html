1970 — 2020

The Hunter House Foundation stewards the residence Richard Hunter designed for his family at 203 Goward Road, Prospect Lake, and revised continuously across more than five decades. The archive catalogues drawings, photographs and related materials documenting this fifty-year working record — schemes abandoned, permits resubmitted, rooms built and removed, ideas held in reserve for a decade before reappearing in built form.

The archive is open and queryable. The catalogue is maintained as a Wikibase; this site is the public reading room.

§The Foundation

The Hunter House Foundation stewards the residence and the archival legacy of its architect. Its overarching aim is to realize the speculative futures embedded in Hunter's drawings — among them a cantilevered Skywalk and the unfinished East Wing developments — and to open the archive to scholars, students and the public.
