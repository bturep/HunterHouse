Hunter House
Foundation

An initiative dedicated to the stewardship of the residence and architectural archive of architect Richard Morrow Hunter (1930–2023). 203 Goward Road, Saanich, British Columbia.

Status
Work in progress This site is being assembled. The archive itself is live and growing; the surrounding pages — Hunter, the House, and the Foundation’s mandate — are still being written.
The archive

A structured catalogue of the drawings, photographs, correspondence, and related materials of Richard Hunter’s practice.

Materials are drawn from six source collections and held on Wikibase — the open structured-data platform that underlies Wikidata — openly queryable through SPARQL and a JSON API. The browser is a working research tool: filter by collection, date, area, or project phase, read each item’s record, and view high-resolution scans.
