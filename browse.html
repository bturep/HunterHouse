Hunter House
Foundation Archive

A foundation dedicated to the stewardship of the residence and architectural archive of architect Richard Morrow Hunter (1930–2023).

203 Goward Road, Saanich, British Columbia

Open data archive on Wikibase — the knowledge graph software underlying Wikidata. Items persistent and individually addressable by URI; statements are typed RDF triples carrying qualifiers, references, and ranks; the catalogue is SPARQL-queryable and federable against external Linked Open Data endpoints. Archival description follows GLAM conventions and the CCA Digital Archives Manual — multi-level arrangement at fonds, series, file, and item. Items aligned to external identifiers (Q-IDs) for cross-catalogue reconciliation.

Structured metadata: CC0, per Wikibase/Wikidata convention. Content — drawings, documents, photographs: CC BY-NC-ND 4.0, rights granted by Frances Hunter. Material held by the Canadian Architectural Archives — University of Calgary remains under separate stewardship; contact the CAA for use.
